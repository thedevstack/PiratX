<h1>PiratX – Simple, Clear, Reliable</h1>

<p>This XMPP messenger is built on the proven projects <b>Monocles Chat</b>, <b>Cheogram</b>, and <b>Conversations</b>, combining their strengths in a clean and user-friendly app.</p>

<h2>Highlights</h2>
<ul>
  <li><b>Easy to Use</b>: No cluttered menus, no unnecessary clicks</li>
  <li><b>Proven Foundation</b>: Stable and reliable thanks to well-tested open-source projects</li>
  <li><b>Focus on the Essentials</b>: Removed meaningless or experimental features</li>
  <li><b>XMPP</b>: Open, federated protocol – free from central control</li>
</ul>

<h2>Why this app?</h2>
<p>Because you want a messenger that simply works, is easy to use, and builds on the best XMPP implementations.</p>

<p>No clutter.<br>
No experiments.<br>
<b>Just communication at its best.</b></p>
